Description

This is a tasty South African pudding best served with warm custard. Never tried it? Let me not tell you all about it, but let it speak for itself with your palette.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon apricot jam
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

Steps for preparation

  1. Preheat oven to (200 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9-inch cake pan.
  2. Add the sugar, egg, and butter into a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until creamy. Beat in apricot jam, vinegar, and baking soda. Add milk and flour; beat until batter is smooth. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, this should take about 35 minutes.
But wait... remember I said it tastes amazing with custard? So we need to learn how to make our own home made creamy custard goodness. So we are not yet done!

Custard ingredients

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

Steps for preparation

  1. Place milk, vanilla extract, and butter in a saucepan. Cook and stir over medium heat until simmering; remove from heat before it comes to a boil.
  2. Separate the egg white from the egg white and yolk
  3. Mix the egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch together in a bowl until sugar dissolves and creates a yellow paste.
  4. Set the saucepan back over low heat. Pour in egg mixture slowly, whisking constantly, until custard thickens enough to coat the bottom of a spoon this should take about 5 to 10 minutes.
