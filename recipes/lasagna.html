Lasagna

Description

Chicken lasagna is a fantastic family meal filled with cheesy goodness and high in protein, I highly recommend you try this recipe out next time you feel a mighty hunger.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Broth
  • Milk
We obviously can not forget the seasoning for some flavour!
  • Salt
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
    • Black pepper
  • fresh parsley
For our cheese option you can choose any of the options below:
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Ricotta
It wouldn't be a chicken lasagna recipe without.... Chicken! So go along and get some chicken breast.

Steps for preparation

  1. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the onion and garlic in butter, then whisk in the flour. Add the broth, milk, and salt.
  3. Add some of the mozzarella and Parmesan. Season with basil, oregano, and pepper. Set aside.
  4. Assemble the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven
